WELCOME TO PETMART

Caring for Your Furry Family Members

Petmart is India's most trusted and beloved pet care destination. Since 1996, we've been dedicated to providing the highest quality products and services for dogs, cats, birds, fish, and small animals. Our journey began with a simple mission: to help pet parents give their furry friends the happiest, healthiest lives possible.

We offer premium pet food from trusted international and local brands, durable and engaging toys, comfortable bedding and accessories, and professional grooming services. Every product in our store is carefully selected and vet-approved to ensure it meets the highest standards of safety and nutrition.

Our team of experienced veterinarians, pet nutritionists, and groomers work together to provide expert advice and personalized care for your beloved companions. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or have years of experience, Petmart is here to support you every step of the way.
